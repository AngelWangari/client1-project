Added Advantages

1.Standard Operating Procedures (SOP’S).

Since ‘a clean environment” is so relative, we have gone an extra mile to make sure that we set standards which to follow. All our stewards are inducted and trained to follow our Standard Operating Procedures when doing their duties. These are contained in our training manual. The procedures enable each cleaner to carry out his or her duties effectively. They also give us a measuring rod which to use when accessing if the cleaner has done his job right and to the expected standard. It also gives you our client a standard to use when appraising us. As a result of this, when we at Xpat say ‘a environment is clean’ this means that it has met certain standards.


2.Check Lists

This is a tool used by the cleaner to attain the standards mentioned above. We have various checklists depending on the area, frequency of cleaning and sensitivity.

3. Partnership in maintenance of Fixtures

As our cleaners go about their normal duties, they have been trained to note any fixtures and fittings not working properly. When this is noted our supervisor fills a form and forwards it to our client for action. The form contains information on where the problem is and what is not working properly. This helps the property managers to repair any items quickly.

4. Employees

Candidates must be over 21 years of age with a minimum O-level certificate and a valid Kenyan ID. Tea stewards require a Food Handling Certificate from a public health facility. All applicants must provide a Certificate of Good Conduct, demonstrate proficiency in English and Kiswahili, have contactable referees, and undergo mandatory induction training covering confidentiality, customer service, hygiene, grooming, PR, and etiquette. Uniforms with badges are required.


5.Training of Stuff

Xpat Products Services LTD support staff undergoes internal & external training in order to keep a breast with market requirement and ever changing customer needs Our Internal training takes place every quarter and external training is done once a year through manufacturers, distributors or umbrella bodies. All staffs are trained on all safety measures as regards to usage of the products, use of protective devices and First Aid skills.

At Xpat Products Services LTD nothing is left to chance and that is why we have engage specialists for all our training. Part of the training covers the following;-

  • Product composition and use.

  • First aid skills.

  • Disposal of wastes.

  • Protective clothing.

  • Current trends in house keeping and beautification.

  • Customer relationship.
