Job details:

Xpat Ltd is always on the lookout for motivated, skilled, and dedicated individuals to join our diverse team. We offer a wide range of opportunities across various fields, including cleaning services, sanitary solutions, landscaping and beautification, and outsourced roles such as janitors, receptionists, messengers, and tea stewards. Additionally, we welcome professionals in supply chain management to assist with the procurement and distribution of office consumables, cleaning materials, detergents, toiletries, chemicals, disinfection products, safety products (PPEs), and non-pharmaceutical medical consumables. If you are passionate about providing high-quality services and contributing to a team that values excellence and customer satisfaction, Xpat Ltd could be the perfect place for you.

Major responsibilities include maintaining confidentiality, ensuring excellent customer service, upholding personal hygiene and grooming standards, and fostering positive public relations while representing the company professionally at all times.

  • Deliver high-quality services in your designated role.
  • Maintain professionalism and adhere to company protocols.
  • Collaborate with team members to ensure smooth operations.
  • Adapt to various tasks as needed.
  • Demonstrate strong work ethic and reliability.

  • Must be over 21 years of age and minimum O-level certificate with a valid Kenyan ID
  • Tea stewards must provide certificate of Food handling from public Health facility
  • All must provide certificate of Good Conduct from the National Police Service
  • Updated Service Record Forms are kept for all our employees
  • Must be proficient in both oral and written English and Kiswahili.
  • Must undergo initial mandatory induction training before deployment that includes confidentiality, customer service, personal hygiene, grooming, PR, etiquette among others
  • Each must have contactable referees
  • Must have neat uniforms complete with badges
