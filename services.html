Our Service Procedures

1. Carpet Shampooing

All carpeted areas shall be cleaned using carpet shampoo. Non-fixed equipments and furniture that exists in each carpeted area shall be removed prior to shampooing. Once equipments have been removed from area carpets shall be shampooed starting at area furthest from door working slowly to the door to ensure that workers do not have to walk on already cleaned area. Carpets shall be left to dry and once dry all equipments and furniture shall be returned to its original place. The carpeted areas will be shampooed once a month as routine, or as necessary depending on their conditions and also with the instructions of the client.

2. Vacuuming Carpets

Carpeted areas shall be vacuumed free from all loose solids, debris and spots/stains removed. Furniture or other equipment moved during vacuuming carpets shall be returned to their original positions. This exercise will be done on a daily basis.

3. PVC/Terrazzo Floor Surfaces

All carpeted areas shall be cleaned using carpet shampoo. Non-fixed equipments and furniture that exists in each carpeted area shall be removed prior to shampooing. Once equipments have been removed from area carpets shall be shampooed starting at area furthest from door working slowly to the door to ensure that workers do not have to walk on already cleaned area. Carpets shall be left to dry and once dry all equipments and furniture shall be returned to its original place. The carpeted areas will be shampooed once a month as routine, or as necessary depending on their conditions and also with the instructions of the client.

4. Ceramic Floor and Wall Surfaces

The ceramic floor surfaces and walls mainly in the washrooms will be cleaned using suitable Ceramic Solution and rinsed thoroughly with water, cleaned and disinfected on a daily basis. We will ensure the floors and the walls are clean and dry all the time.

5. Cemented floor surfaces

These floor surfaces will be damp mopped daily with multipurpose soap in water, kept clean and dry at all the times. Once a week the floor will be machine scrubbed and buffed. The floors will be kept dry all the time.

6. Receptions Area

Floor surfaces in these areas will be mopped continuously. One dedicated cleaner will stationed at the reception area. These areas will be machine buffed and polished on a weekly basis. The standard floor maintenance procedure described above will be applicable. The floor area will be kept dry and clean at all time. Walls in these areas will be wiped and dusted daily.

7. Offices and Meeting Rooms

All the offices and fittings will be cleaned first thing in the morning using approved detergents, and polished once a week. Cleaning, polishing and machine buffing will be done frequently and stains removed continuously from all the areas. All the carpeted offices will be vacuum- cleaned regularly and carpets shampooed once a month or as per the client’s instructions.

8. Furniture and Fittings

All the furniture and fittings will be dusted. Top range sprays- Pledge and Mr. Sheen will be used for polishing of desks on soft clothes. This will leave the desks and furniture shinny. The door frames will be dusted during this exercise. Fabric seats/velvet seats/sofa sets will be dusted during the cleaning exercise.

9. Sweeping/Dust Mopping

Concrete, PVC, and Terrazzo tile flooring will be swept and damp mopped so as to remove all loose dirt, streaks or smears. Furniture or equipment moved during floor cleaning shall be returned to original position.

10. Glass Mirrors

The glass mirrors will be cleaned using windowlene once a week. Finger marks on the mirrors will be removed constantly.

11. Office Cleaning

All wastebaskets shall be emptied and ashtrays and ash receptacles washed each morning of the workday. All furniture, desk, chairs, bookcases, books, air conditioners, fans, equipment and horizontal hedges and sills shall be dusted. Walls and partitions shall be wiped clean to a height of seven feet above floor level, including clocks and pictures. UPS's, PC's, printers, monitors, will be cleaned with light damp cloth. Glass screen of monitors will be cleaned with glass cleaner solution only. Furniture or other item moved during cleaning shall be returned to their original position.

12. Wash Room

These will be disinfected after every hour. We will use approved disinfectants and Glade will be supplied in the toilets. Multicolor urinal balls will be placed in the urinals to maintain a constant freshness in the washrooms. The Basins, Sinks and urinals will be disinfected twice daily including the flash and taps. The mirrors in the washrooms will be cleaned twice a day.

13. Sanitary fittings - (Sinks / Toilet Bowls / Seat Bidets)

All hand touch facilities including the sinks, toilet bowls, seat bidets have a porcelain finish, they will be scrubbed, dusted and cleaned using a disinfectant soap and scotch bright on a daily basis. The doorframes will be dusted and cleaned during this exercise. All the hand touch facilities in your toilets will be disinfected twice on a daily basis.

14. Dustbins

Dustbins will be emptied twice in a day i.e. in the Morning and in the evening. This will be dumped in one central location ready for collection. These areas will however be kept clean. Cleaning and disinfecting of the bins will depend on the type of bin on a weekly basis

15. Glass Windows / Partitions

The internal windows will be cleaned using Windowlene once a week. Finger marks from the doors and windows will be removed constantly. Dusting of the windows will be done on a daily basis.

16. Notice Board/ Fire Equipments/Closets

All the notice boards with glass sliding doors and metal frames will be wiped and dusted daily using windowlene. Soft notice boards will also be dusted daily, cobwebs removed, birds’ nests and wasp nests removed constantly. The fire equipment closest with glass will be wiped and kept clean.

17. Walls, Pillars and Ceilings

The walls, pillars and ceiling in all the premises will be dusted on a daily basis. Removal of stains will be done weekly or as necessary using appropriate spot cleaner any dampness noted on the ceilings will be promptly reported to the premise representative/caretaker. Removal of dust and cobwebs will be done once a week.

18. Staircases and Corridors

All corridors and staircases in the common areas will be swept and mopped daily and floor will be kept dry throughout the day. The floor surfaces in the staircase areas will be addressed following the standard floor maintenance procedures described above. Scrubbing and stripping will be done on a weekly basis

19. Parking, External Walkways and Surroundings

These areas will be swept every day early in the morning and litter collected and disposed appropriately. The paved surfaces will be cleaned using Hard Brooms, Water and detergent once every two weeks. All the verandahs will be swept, washed weekly and kept dry all the times.
